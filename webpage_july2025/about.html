About Mamawmail

Building Resilient Communication

Mamawmail is an open-source project revolutionizing decentralized messaging, ensuring secure and reliable communication in disconnected environments like disaster zones and remote enterprises.

Our Mission

Mamawmail aims to provide resilient, privacy-focused messaging for areas with limited or no internet connectivity. Using a serverless swarm architecture, AI-driven path optimization, and fractal propagation, we ensure messages reach their destination with minimal latency. Features like delete_after_read for privacy, hop_ack for delivery confirmation, and been_here for path efficiency make Mamawmail a robust solution for critical communication needs.

Our Team

Led by Eng Juan Carlos G. Ayeng, Mamawmail is driven by a passion for decentralized technology and community collaboration. We are an open-source project welcoming contributors from around the world to join our swarm and build the future of messaging.

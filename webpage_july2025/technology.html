Mamawmail Technology

Decentralized, Secure, Resilient

Mamawmail leverages a serverless swarm architecture, AI-driven path optimization, and robust privacy mechanisms to deliver messages in disconnected environments with minimal latency.

Technical Foundation

Mamawmail’s technology is built on a decentralized, serverless swarm that routes messages dynamically across devices. Using federated learning, our AI optimizes paths (targeting \( P_{\text{opt}} \to 1 \)), while features like delete_after_read ensure privacy, hop_ack confirms delivery, and been_here prevents redundant routing. Fractal propagation scales efficiently, making Mamawmail ideal for disaster zones and remote enterprises.
